Welcome to "Bee"ing Mindful Of The Bees. Our agency is here to help you with any information you may need on how to better protect and be more mindful of our important pollinating friends, the bees.

Bees are important to the ecosystem and you!

Bees not only make great honey, they account for over 1/3 of our food supply and pollinate food from the cherry blossom tree, potatoes, and all sorts of beautiful flowers too. They are also in critical danger of habitat destruction and poisoning from pesticides!

For more information on protecting your backyard bees call us at (817) 999-1695.

Bees are an integral part of the ecosystem; and we are now about to dive into the secret world of bees and why they need to be protected.

Honey bees are critical to U.S. food supplies. 1/3 of the foods we eat are pollinated by honey bees, especially the good stuff (our "superfoods" rich in vitamins and nutrients). They are the tiny forces behind more than $20 billion worth of U.S. crops a year and they are in danger!>

These busy bees need our help staying protected from things such as toxic pesticide such as neonics, overuse of nature's resources, drought, and habitat destruction. Although things such as wildfires can be natural and good for the ecosystem, our interference is not natural and gaining materials such as lumber without regard for the balance, can severely harm the ability for that same land to give back what is taken; to replenish and nurture itself. Things such as recycling help protect those trees and the habitat that effects the ecosystem the bees depend on. We must become more mindful of the role we play and the resources we may dispose of irresponsibly. Please become more mindful and help us protect the bees! 🐝
